Social Media Came here claiming to get rid of the Social Hegemony of who get's to express their Opinions. At least that's how it got marketed here in India. It I think started as a PostModern Phenomenon, it sought to get rid of the " CRITICS CLASS ", a seperate set of people who were claimed to have held authority and knowledge over what is right and wrong and were said to have got the privelage opinion. But i don't think that is the case anymore , especially after almost 2 decades of social media we can surely say , it has watered down opinions and Criticism to a very diluted state, that it has lost the power it claimed to have guaranteed to each and every user. It is the algorithm that holds the power of choosing which opinions to prioritise for whom. It all i think startes from Mr.Barthes [Death Of The Author] (https://www.ubu.com/aspen/aspen5and6/threeEssays.html#barthes) . From my reading i see that he has managed to neither Empover the Reader nor Destroy the Author's self, instead he has given a death blow to the Critic , who earlier defined or try to understand the places of and the relationship between the Author and the Reader. Now after the Critic got killed , the relationship dynamic and the critical consensus the Critic held from his supposed deep readings were watered down to the level of a layman who had no previous encounter with the medium (depending on the medium) and only came across the text just at the moment. Now this is where the problem arises.
How do we define whose opinion holds more value over other ?
